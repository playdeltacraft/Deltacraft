How to join the Deltacraft Server:

1. Open your Delta Outlook and start an email.

Log in to your school-provided email client. Compose a message to ahoy@mcdeltat.net.



2. Verify Yourself.

As a layer of security, players can only join the server once they've been verified as a member of Delta College. Provide your (first and last) name, Minecraft username, and whether you'll be playing on Java or Bedrock. You'll be added to the whitelist.



3. Play!

Once reviewed, you'll receive an email back with confirmation. You'll be given the server address, port, and an invite to the Discord server.
