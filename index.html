How to join the Deltacraft Server:

1. Open your Delta Outlook and start an email.

Log in to your school-provided email client. Compose a message to ahoy@mcdeltat.net.



2. Verify Yourself.

As a layer of security, players can only join the server once they've been verified as a member of Delta College.

Provide your first and last name, Minecraft username, and whether you'll be playing on Java or Bedrock.

*IMPORTANT: As this Minecraft server is technically a part of a club, school policy requires me to send in a list of members. It's your choice whether or not you want your information shared with Delta. If you'd like to be a member of the Minecraft club, please include this in your email!



3. Play!

Once reviewed, your Minecraft account will be whitelisted and you'll receive an email back with confirmation.

You'll be given the server address, port, and an invite to the Discord server.
